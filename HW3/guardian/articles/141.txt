Tell us your experiences testing for the coronavirus

To ease the strain on Public Health England, NHS hospital laboratories will test swab samples from people who display symptoms of coronavirus<U+FEFF>.
We want to hear from people working for PHE and NHS 111 about their experiences of testing people for coronavirus. How do you think the process is going? What are the challenges for staff?
