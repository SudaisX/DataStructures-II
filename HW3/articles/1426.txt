Retired health workers: would you return to work to help with coronavirus?

Over 65,000 letters to NHS retirees have been sent out asking them to return to work to help deal with the coronavirus outbreak.
In an interview with BBC Breakfast on Friday, health secretary Matt Hancock said to those in retirement: “Your NHS needs you.”
But do former doctors, surgeons, nurses and other health professionals wish to interrupt their retirement? What do you feel about the wording of Matt Hancock’s request?
